# Dummy Investor — Content Diagnosis
Date: April 24, 2026
Based on: Instagram Insights (Mar 25–Apr 23), 4 sample reels, cover grid

## The numbers — last 30 days

- Total views: 1,669
- Accounts reached: 1,412 (down 31.3%)
- Content posted: 20 pieces (99.3% Reels, 0.7% Stories)
- Average views per post: ~83
- Followers who saw content: 5.8%
- Non-followers who saw content: 94.2%
- **New followers: 0**
- Profile visits: 21 (down 66.7%)
- External link taps: **0**
- Interactions total: 37 (across 20 posts — under 2 per post)

One post visible in the grid got only **5 views** ("IT'S TIME"). That's a likely algorithmic suppression — music rights, flagged content, or a broken upload. Needs investigation.

## What the numbers actually say

This is the most important insight: **Instagram IS serving your content to non-followers.** 94% non-follower reach means the algorithm is testing you and giving you chances. You are not shadow-banned. The problem is downstream.

What's broken:
1. **Viewer → follower conversion:** 1,412 unique people saw content. 0 followed. That's a content or profile problem, not a reach problem.
2. **Viewer → profile click:** only 21 profile visits from 1,412 reach = 1.5%. People watch and keep scrolling.
3. **Profile → link click:** 21 profile visits, 0 link taps. Even the tiny slice that was curious didn't feel pulled to the book/course.

Instagram is literally handing him chances. He's not converting them.

## The root cause — brand/execution mismatch

The brand promise (from his own document): "calm, mature, not FOMO, not gurus, slow wealth > fast mistakes."

The actual covers in the last 30 days: CRISIS, STOP, CEASEFIRE, BAD NEWS, TOXIC, IT'S A LIE, IT ALL?, YOUR BRAIN IS COSTING. Every cover features a shocked/worried face expression. Every caption is panic-coded.

The actual videos (based on 4 samples): calm studio talk, professional setup, measured delivery, no pattern interrupt in the opening frame, captions start mid-sentence (MOST / WHY DOWN BY / WHEN MARKETS / EVERYTHING — none of these tell the viewer what they're about to hear).

The result is the worst of both worlds:
- Panic-coded covers attract viewers who want drama → they get calm studio talk → they scroll in 2 seconds.
- Calm/thoughtful viewers who'd love the real content scroll past because the covers look like every other finance panic-creator.

The algorithm sees poor retention and stops pushing. That's why the 20 posts plateau at 80–150 views.

## What this is NOT

- It is not a reach problem. The algo is giving him reach.
- It is not a production quality problem. The studio, lighting, mic, and captions are genuinely professional.
- It is not a topic problem at the strategic level — long-term investing, crashes, patience, DCA, inflation are all solid.
- It is not a posting frequency problem at this scale.

## What this IS

A **hook + cover + first-3-seconds problem.** This is where 100% of the fix happens.

## Concrete fixes

### Covers (top priority)
- Kill the shocked-face-every-time template. It's become noise.
- Test 3 cover styles in parallel over 2 weeks:
  - **Style A — Single-number cover:** large number + short claim ("$100/mo for 30 years = $349K"). No face.
  - **Style B — Text-first cover:** bold statement, muted background, small face in corner ("Most people start investing too late. Here's why.")
  - **Style C — Chart-as-hook cover:** actual S&P chart or growth curve as main visual, short caption overlay.
- No more CRISIS / STOP / TOXIC / BAD NEWS wording. That contradicts the brand. Every panic word used is a subscriber the brand never gets.

### Hooks (first 3 seconds of the actual video)
Current pattern: open in studio, start mid-sentence, caption is a fragment.
New pattern to test:
- **First frame must contain a full statement or a number** — not a partial caption. Example: open on a caption that says "If you invested $100/month since 1990…" before he even speaks.
- **First spoken word must be a question or a promise** — "Most people think crashes destroy wealth. They're wrong." Then cut to him.
- **Use B-roll or a chart for the first 1 second** instead of his face, especially on non-follower reach. His face works after the hook lands, not as the hook itself.

### The "5-view" post
Find it and check what's wrong. Music copyright, geopolitical flag, muted auto-hide. If you have 19 posts getting 80–150 and one getting 5, that's a signal, not randomness.

### CTA and bio
0 link taps means the bio isn't pulling people to anything they want. Need to:
- Rewrite bio to be benefit-driven, not descriptive.
- Pick ONE link in the bio for the next 30 days, not a link tree of everything.
- Add an explicit CTA in the last 2 seconds of every Reel pointing to that one link.

## What we are NOT doing yet

- No paid ads. No point boosting content that isn't converting organically.
- No new content formats (carousels, Stories campaigns) until the Reels fix works.
- No course launch or book launch while the content engine is broken — we'd be launching to a non-converting audience.

## Success metrics for the next 30 days

Realistic targets, not vanity:
- Average views per post: 80 → 250+
- New followers: 0 → 30+
- Profile visits: 21 → 100+
- Link taps: 0 → 15+
- One post breaking 1,000 views

If these don't move, the fix isn't working and we re-diagnose.
