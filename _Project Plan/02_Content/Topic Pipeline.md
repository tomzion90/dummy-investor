## How I pick the next topic

When you say "let's make a reel," I:
1. Look at the most recent Weekly Review (if any) to see what's performing.
2. Check which pillar the last 3 Reels came from — I balance rotation so the feed looks coherent.
3. Pick the highest-priority unproduced topic that makes sense next.
4. Tell you the topic, pillar, and hook angle before I start producing. You can veto.
