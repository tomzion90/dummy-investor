# Master Timeline — Dummy Investor

**Starting point:** April 27, 2026 (Week 1)
**Time budget:** 6–8 hours/week
**Budget:** 500–1,000 NIS initial, reinvested from sales

This is the single master timeline across all workstreams. Every agent's work fits inside this plan. When in doubt, this file wins.

The plan has 4 phases, each with clear exit criteria. We don't advance to the next phase unless the prior phase's criteria are met — otherwise we'd be building on broken foundations.

---

## Phase 1 — Reset the Content Engine + Fix the Book
**Weeks 1–4 (Apr 27 – May 24)**
**Exit criteria:** Avg views per Reel > 200. At least 15 new followers in 30 days. Book editorial + layout passes complete.

### Week 1 (Apr 27 – May 3)
- **Content:** Brand Bible + Hook Library written (1 session). Bio + single link rebuilt (1 session). First Reel produced with new system (1 session). Owner records and posts 2 Reels using the new covers/hooks.
- **Book:** Audience Decision pass — pick kids vs tween vs adult-parent (1 session).
- **Analytics:** Baseline screenshot captured in PROJECT_STATE.

### Week 2 (May 4 – May 10)
- **Content:** 3 new Reels produced and posted. First weekly review.
- **Book:** Editorial pass — full typo and rewrite list (1 session).

### Week 3 (May 11 – May 17)
- **Content:** 3 new Reels. Weekly review.
- **Book:** Layout pass — trim size decision, background image fix, margin/font spec (1 session).
- **Funnel:** Landing page v1 for bio link destination (1 session).

### Week 4 (May 18 – May 24)
- **Content:** 3 new Reels. Weekly review.
- **Book:** Cover brief written and sent to Fiverr designer ($80–120). Owner hires.
- **Check-in:** Phase 1 exit review. Are views and followers moving? If not, diagnose before advancing.

## Phase 2 — Launch the Book + Scale Content
**Weeks 5–8 (May 25 – Jun 21)**
**Exit criteria:** Book live on Amazon with at least 5 sales or reviews. Avg views per Reel > 400. Email list started with 50+ subscribers.

### Week 5 (May 25 – May 31)
- **Content:** 3 new Reels. Weekly review.
- **Book:** Receive Fiverr cover, review, request revisions if needed.
- **Funnel:** Email capture active, welcome email sequence written (1 session).

### Week 6 (Jun 1 – Jun 7)
- **Content:** 3 new Reels. Weekly review.
- **Book:** Listing copy — title, subtitle, 7 keywords, 2 categories, description, A+ content (1 session).
- **Book:** Final manuscript upload to KDP (owner action).

### Week 7 (Jun 8 – Jun 14)
- **Content:** 3 new Reels — 1 of them starts seeding the book ("the book about X is coming in Y days").
- **Book:** KDP proof copy ordered and reviewed.
- **Funnel:** Book buyer email sequence written.

### Week 8 (Jun 15 – Jun 21)
- **BOOK LAUNCH** 🎯 — goes live on Amazon.
- **Content:** 3 launch-focused Reels. Ask existing followers to buy.
- **Funnel:** Book landing page updated to purchase flow.
- **Check-in:** Phase 2 exit review. Book is live. Content is scaling. Email list exists.

## Phase 3 — Design the Course
**Weeks 9–12 (Jun 22 – Jul 19)**
**Exit criteria:** Course fully scripted. Recording setup validated. Pre-launch waitlist open with at least 100 signups.

### Week 9 (Jun 22 – Jun 28)
- **Course:** Unpauses. Syllabus finalized (1 session). Pricing + positioning decision (1 session).
- **Content:** 3 Reels. Weekly review. One Reel teases the course is coming.
- **Funnel:** Course waitlist landing page (1 session).

### Week 10 (Jun 29 – Jul 5)
- **Course:** Intro + Lesson 1 scripted (1 session each). Slides outlined.
- **Content:** 3 Reels. Weekly review.

### Week 11 (Jul 6 – Jul 12)
- **Course:** Lessons 2–4 scripted.
- **Content:** 3 Reels. Weekly review.

### Week 12 (Jul 13 – Jul 19)
- **Course:** Lessons 5–7 scripted. Full course outline complete.
- **Content:** 3 Reels. Weekly review.
- **Check-in:** Phase 3 exit review. Course is fully scripted. Waitlist has 100+ signups.

## Phase 4 — Record, Edit, Launch the Course
**Weeks 13–20 (Jul 20 – Sep 13)**
**Exit criteria:** Course live, first 10 sales closed.

### Weeks 13–14 (Jul 20 – Aug 2)
- **Course:** Record all lessons over 2 weekends. Use existing studio.
- **Content:** 3 Reels/week rolls on.

### Weeks 15–16 (Aug 3 – Aug 16)
- **Course:** Edit lessons. B-roll, captions, chapter markers.
- **Content:** 3 Reels/week. Add course teasers every 7 posts.

### Week 17 (Aug 17 – Aug 23)
- **Course:** Upload to platform (Teachable / Podia / Thinkific — pick before Week 15).
- **Course:** Sales page live.
- **Funnel:** Course sales email sequence live (5 emails over 7 days).

### Week 18 (Aug 24 – Aug 30)
- **COURSE SOFT LAUNCH** to waitlist only. Limited spots, 20% discount.

### Week 19 (Aug 31 – Sep 6)
- **COURSE PUBLIC LAUNCH** 🎯. All content points to it. Daily stories.

### Week 20 (Sep 7 – Sep 13)
- **Course launch week close.** Last-chance discount. Testimonials collected from early buyers.
- **Check-in:** Final exit review. First 10 sales closed.

## Weekly cadence (every week, all phases)

- **Sunday or Monday:** Weekly review session (Analytics agent).
- **Monday:** Weekly planning session (Content Strategist).
- **Tuesday–Thursday:** Reel production sessions (3 per week).
- **Friday:** Book/Course/Funnel work depending on phase.
- **Saturday:** Owner records Reels, posts if scheduled.

## Rules

- No phase advances without meeting exit criteria. If Phase 1 numbers aren't moving in week 4, we extend Phase 1, not forge ahead.
- No adding new workstreams mid-phase. If a new idea appears, park it in the Parking Lot in `Topic Pipeline.md`.
- Every Friday, PROJECT_STATE.md gets updated with phase status.
